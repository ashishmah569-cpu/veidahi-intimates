From Establishment to Brand

Veidahi began in 2019 with a vision to create something meaningful. What started as a small establishment slowly evolved into a brand driven by passion and persistence.

The journey was not easy. Challenges, losses and uncertainty were part of the process. But every setback helped us grow stronger and smarter.

We focused on improving quality, building trust and understanding what women truly want — comfort, confidence and elegance.

Today, Veidahi Intimates stands as a symbol of growth, strength and modern femininity.

Why Veidahi Exists

To create lingerie that feels as good as it looks — premium, comfortable and empowering.

The foundation was built with courage — the future will be built with vision.
